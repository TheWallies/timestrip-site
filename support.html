TimeStrip Support

World Clock Time Zones

How do I add a city?
Tap the + button in the top right of the main screen. Search for any city by name and tap to add it.
How do I remove a city?
Swipe left on any city card to reveal the delete button. You can also swipe all the way left to delete instantly.
What is Golden Hour?
Golden Hour finds the time window where work hours overlap across your cities. It's shown as red dotted lines on the time bars. Useful for finding meeting times across time zones.
How do I change work hours?
Tap the "Work" tag next to any city name to edit that city's work hours. You can also change your home city's work hours from Settings.
How do I scrub to a different time?
Drag your finger along any time bar to shift all cities to that time. Tap "Now" or wait a moment to snap back to the current time.
How do I set up a widget?
Long-press your Home Screen, tap +, search for "TimeStrip", and choose a size. The widget automatically shows your cities from the app. You can also configure specific cities per widget.
My widget shows the wrong cities
Try removing the widget and adding it again. If it still shows wrong cities, open the TimeStrip app once — this refreshes the widget data.
Does TimeStrip collect my data?
No. TimeStrip does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
