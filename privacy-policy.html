Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

TimeStrip ("the App") is built with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

Summary

TimeStrip does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, use, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data (see below)
  • Contacts, photos, or other device data

Location Services

The App may request access to your device's location services solely to suggest your home city during initial setup. This is entirely optional. If granted, your location is used once, on-device, and is never transmitted to any server. You can deny location access and manually select your city instead.

Data Storage

Your city selections, work hour preferences, and app settings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults and App Group containers). This data is not transmitted anywhere and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Widgets

TimeStrip widgets read your city and settings data from the shared App Group container on your device. No network requests are made by the widget.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since the App collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
